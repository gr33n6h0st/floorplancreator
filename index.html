Prepared by: Brandon Smart

Course Code: TDJ3M

Ontario Curriculum

01 / OVERVIEW

A lightweight CAD engine for the modern classroom.

Floorplancreator.net bridges the gap between manual sketching and heavy industrial software. It functions as a feedback engine, allowing students to focus on design logic rather than software complexity.

Feature: Smart-Snap Interface

[IMAGE: A close-up screenshot of two walls meeting or a window being placed in the software]

Automated wall joints and exact dimension inputs ensure professional-grade scalability.

Feature: Instant 3D Visualization

[IMAGE: A screenshot of your design in 3D mode]

One-click 2D-to-3D transformation to immediately verify ergonomics and sightlines.

02 / RATIONALE

Eliminating Creation Fatigue.

Traditional drafting penalizes mistakes. This tool supports A1 (Ontario Curriculum) by encouraging rapid prototyping. Students can iterate 3x faster than hand-drawing.

Expectation B1: Mastering technical concepts and standard architectural symbols.
