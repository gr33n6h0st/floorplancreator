Grade 11 Technological Design (TDJ3M)

Bridging the Design Gap.

An analysis of Floorplancreator.net as a feedback engine for the modern CAD classroom.

01. Tool Functionality

Floorplancreator.net is a "lightweight" CAD suite that simplifies the technical barriers of architectural drawing.

The browser-based interface allows for seamless transitions between school Chromebooks and home mobile devices, supporting a flexible BYOD (Bring Your Own Device) learning environment.

Smart-Snap Interface

Automated wall joints and exact dimension inputs for professional-grade scalability.

Instant 3D Visualization

One-click 2D-to-3D transformation to immediately verify ergonomics and sightlines.

02. Pedagogical Rationale

Overcoming Creation Fatigue.

Traditional drafting often penalizes mistakes because re-drawing takes hours. This tool allows students to fail fast and iterate quickly. By removing mechanical friction, students shift their cognitive load from merely drawing lines to solving complex design problems.

Curriculum Alignment

  • Expectation A1: Developing and evaluating solutions through rapid prototyping.
  • Expectation B1: Mastering technical concepts and standard architectural symbols.

"This tool functions as a feedback engine, not just a drawing app."
