01 / Tool Functionality

Floorplancreator.net is a "lightweight" CAD suite designed for rapid architectural drafting. Its Smart-Snap interface allows for professional-grade scalability, enabling students to focus on design logic rather than software barriers.

02 / Immersive Verification

The Instant 3D Visualization mode allows students to move from 2D blueprints to a first-person perspective walkthrough. This immediate feedback loop is essential for verifying interior ergonomics and user sightlines.

03 / Pedagogical Rationale

By eliminating "Creation Fatigue," students shift their cognitive load to Ontario Curriculum Expectation A1: developing and evaluating design solutions through rapid prototyping.
