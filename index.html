01 / Technical Workflow & Usability

Floorplancreator.net functions as a lightweight architectural suite. To satisfy Overall Expectation B1 regarding technical concepts, the tool utilizes a specific three-step workflow:

01.

The Smart-Snap System

Users drag walls, windows, and doors onto the canvas. The software automatically heals joints and aligns elements to maintain structural accuracy.

02.

Input Rigor

Unlike simple sketch apps, this tool allows for exact metric or imperial dimension inputs to create scalable technical drawings.

03.

Symbol Literacy

Students must utilize standard architectural symbols, such as door swings and electrical icons, for professional model verification.

02 / Feedback Engine

Instant 3D Visualization

The "Killer Feature" is the one-click 2D-to-3D transformation. This allows for instant ergonomic verification and sightline analysis. By removing the creation fatigue of manual drafting, students focus on Expectation A1: evaluating solutions through rapid prototyping.
